M A S T E R M I N D

Mastermind is a code-breaking game, where the purpose of the game is to guess the code within the specified amount of attempts. The rules are the following:

The player will make a guess of a sequence of four colors, after which the guess and its accuracy will pop up on the board. The accuracy logic is as follows:

  • Black: Depending on the amount of black, one or more of the colors is correct and in the correct position
  • White: Depending on the amount of white, one or more of the colors is correct, but not in the correct position
  • Nothing: Depending on the amount of unupdated pins, one or more of the colors is not in the sequence

The code will be randomly generated and the user will have 6 attempts to guess the code.
